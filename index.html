Minecraft Transit Railway

A Minecraft mod for building fully-fleged, autonomous transport network with built-in System Map and more!

Download Join Discord Read the wiki

Features

Ease of Use

The fundemental features and concept of the Minecraft Transit Railway Mod is designed to be simple and intuitive.
More complex features like signalling is available, but is not a requirement to understand it all in one go.


We also have YouTube tutorials for newcomers to get started!

Real-Time Simulation

Schedule your choice of transport vehicle to automatically depart at any time and frequency you wish with mixed vehicle configurations.


These scheduled vehicles are all automatically simulated in the background, without the need for human interference or keeping any chunks loaded in Minecraft!

Built-in System Map

As your network grows larger, it may become more difficult to keep track of everything.


This mod comes in with a built-in system map accessible with your web browser, which can show a visual representation of your network, check when the next train arrives and more!

Extendable

Apart from the built-in vehicles that is bundled with this mod, you can create your own custom vehicles and signages with Resource Packs.


You may also enjoy a wide variety of community-made contents from various regions!
